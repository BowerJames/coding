The pattern is the durable, language-agnostic design: a provider **returns a stream it fills from a background task** by splitting read/write authority at a seam ‚Äî a single-pass consumer (`AsyncIterator`) and a producer (`push`/`end` writer) linked by one shared queue (`_Core`), paired by a `create_stream()` factory that returns `(consumer, writer)` so the provider keeps the writer and hands back the read side only.
